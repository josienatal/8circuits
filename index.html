What if the way you experience life isn't random, but programmed? Eight circuits. Most people never activate beyond the fourth.

The premise

Leary mapped eight neurological circuits — four terrestrial circuits that keep you alive and functional, four cosmic circuits that most people never access.

The model is speculative, not neuroscience. But the questions it generates are real. Where are you stuck? What's waiting to be unlocked?
